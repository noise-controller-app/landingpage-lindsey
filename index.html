NoiseController

Making classrooms quiet across America

NoiseController is: a handy app that uses your device's microphone to gauge the noise level of students in your classroom, displaying a slideshow of cute animals when your desired level of quiet is achieved. Be careful though because if it's too loud the animals will be scared away. NoiseController saves educators time(and throat losenges) by allowing users to: set custom noise tolerance levels have a live tally of quiet minutes for the period and a final score for the day, you can even tally quiet time over the term Easily set up users by classroom, class or instructor name for your whole institution
